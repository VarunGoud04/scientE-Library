Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe online experience.

1. Introduction

At Scient E-Library, we value your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our site:

  • Personal details (e.g., name, email address)
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, interaction with our platform)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (e.g., browser cookies)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, courses, or support requests.
  • To analyze usage and improve the functionality of our website.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except for the following reasons:

  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., a court order).
  • To provide services through trusted partners (e.g., payment processors). These partners are required to protect your data.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at scient.e.library.info@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.
