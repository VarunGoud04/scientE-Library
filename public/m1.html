Access a curated list of important questions for **Matrices and Calculus**, which are vital for your B.Tech exams under the JNTUH curriculum.

Matrices - Important Questions

  • Find the inverse of a matrix using elementary row operations.
  • Find Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors of a given matrix.
  • Characterize diagonalizable matrices and find the diagonal form.
  • Apply Cramer's rule to solve systems of linear equations.

Calculus - Important Questions

  • Evaluate definite and indefinite integrals of standard functions.
  • Find limits and continuity of multivariable functions.
  • Perform differentiation on trigonometric and exponential functions.
  • Application of integration to find areas and volumes of solids of revolution.

Mixed Topics

  • Solution of ordinary differential equations (ODEs) using matrix methods.
  • Application of matrices in solving problems of optimization in calculus.
  • Use of matrix methods in solving higher-order differential equations.
  • Find Taylor and Maclaurin series expansions of matrix functions.
