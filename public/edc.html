Important Questions - EDC

Here you'll find a curated list of important questions for **Electronic Devices and Circuits (EDC)** that will help you excel in your B.Tech exams.

Semiconductors & Diodes

  • Explain the working of a PN-junction diode and its I-V characteristics.
  • Discuss Zener diode characteristics and its use in voltage regulation.
  • Explain the concept of forward and reverse biasing in diodes.
  • Describe the working of a light-emitting diode (LED).

Transistors & Amplifiers

  • Explain the working of a Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT) in common emitter configuration.
  • Discuss the voltage gain of a transistor amplifier.
  • Explain the working of a field-effect transistor (FET) and its characteristics.
  • Describe the working principle of a differential amplifier.

Oscillators & Power Electronics

  • Discuss the working of a Colpitts oscillator.
  • Explain the operation of a phase-shift oscillator.
  • Describe the operation of a SCR (Silicon Control Rectifier) and its application.
  • Explain the working principle of a buck converter.
