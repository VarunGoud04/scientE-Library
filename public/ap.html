Important Questions - Applied Physics

Here you'll find a curated list of important questions for **Applied Physics** that will help you excel in your B.Tech exams.

Mechanics & Fluid Dynamics

  • Derive the equation of motion for a body under uniform acceleration.
  • Explain Bernoulli’s principle and its applications in fluid flow.
  • Discuss the principles of conservation of linear momentum.
  • Explain the working of a hydraulic press and its applications.

Optics & Waves

  • Explain the working principle of a laser.
  • Derive the laws of reflection and refraction.
  • Discuss the principles of interference and diffraction.
  • Explain Doppler effect in sound and light.

Electromagnetism

  • State and explain Maxwell’s equations.
  • Explain the working of an electric motor.
  • Discuss the properties of electromagnetic waves.
  • Explain the concept of electromagnetic induction.
